Book Buddies

More Information:

Book Buddies is a Northwestern student group that partners with local preschools to promote literacy and tutor young people in reading. Weekly meetings every Friday from 3-5pm. Volunteering commitments can be on a weekly basis. The preschools that we split our time between are Childcare Center of Evanston, and Reba Early Learning Center. The children's outreach librarian from the Evanston Public Library attends and helps each week as well.
