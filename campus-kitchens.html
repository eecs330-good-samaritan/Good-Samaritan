Campus Kitchens

More Information:

Campus Kitchens collects over 30,000 lbs of excess food from Northwestern dining halls that would otherwise be thrown away. We package the food into healthy meals and deliver these meals to low-income Evanston residents for free. We are conveniently based on campus in the Allison Hall Dining Hall.

We have volunteer shifts every day of the week - no prior experience is required.
